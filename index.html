About Us

Hello, I am the CEO of GoCart


GoCart Private Limited is an Indian e-commerce company established in 2007. It started with a primary focus on online book sales and soon, expanded to lifestyle products, electronics, home essentials and groceries. Today, GoCart is the biggest online Indian marketplace competing with the another componies. Since 2010, the company has made a number of acquisitions including Letsbuy, Myntra, Jabong, eBay India, etc. In addition to its main office in different all over Globe, GoCart has branch offices in India and the firm is registered in Singapore. In 2018, the US-based retail chain Walmart acquired majority stake in GoCart. The centre is run by talented engineers from across the world.
